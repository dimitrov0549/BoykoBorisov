Boyko Borissov - Inspiring Leader

Biography

Boyko Borissov is a Bulgarian politician, former Prime Minister and Mayor of Sofia. He was born on June 13, 1959, in Sofia. Borissov is a former police officer and served as the Chief Secretary of the Ministry of Interior. In 2009, he became the Prime Minister of Bulgaria, leading the GERB party.
